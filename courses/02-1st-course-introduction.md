### 1st Course: Introduction


#### 1st Topic: Introduction


**1.1.1.1. The teachings studied here are for receiving the Holy Spirit. God is the one who gives the Holy Spirit, the teachings here show you and make you fit and ready to receive the Holy Spirit. At the end of these teachings, you will realize that you will have received the Holy Spirit unknowingly.**

**1.1.1.2. Who is the Holy Spirit?**
- To those who are Christians, I know that this question is easy for you to answer. The Holy Spirit is simply the righteous Spirit of God that He gave to the world through Jesus Christ.
- (Isaiah 11:2-3 EASY) [2] The Spirit of the Lord will be with him. The Spirit will make him wise, so that he understands things. The Spirit will show him what is right, and he will have the power to do it. The Spirit will cause him to know the Lord and to serve him truly. [3] To obey the Lord will make him very happy. He will not judge people because of what he sees with his eyes. He will not judge them because of what his ears hear about them.

**1.1.1.3. What can the Holy Spirit do?**
- Since the Holy Spirit is God's Spirit, He can do everything except evil.
- (Habakkuk 1:13 EASY) [13] But you are too holy to accept anything that is evil. You cannot agree with people who do wrong things…….
- The Bible has told us that God cannot accept anything that is evil, in the same way, God's Holy Spirit cannot accept to do anything evil.

**1.1.1.4. How will the Holy Spirit help me?**
- This is a question that has no obvious answer, but you can imagine about this;
- (Genesis 1:27 ERV) [27] So God created humans in his own image. He created them to be like himself. He created them male and female.
- God created humans in his own image, but we know that God is a Spirit,
- (John 4:24 ERV) [24] God is spirit. So, the people who worship him must worship in spirit and truth.”
- This verse shows us two connected truths, "God is Spirit", this shows us the nature of God. "…must worship him in spirit and truth." This shows us that those who worship also have a spirit.
- So, God created humans in his own image, but we know that God is a Spirit, it means that God created a spirit in humans. In a human body, there is a spirit that God created. Unfortunately, the spirit in the human body is weak and it can do nothing, when Satan (the spirit that God sent out of heaven) comes he does everything he wishes to the human spirits because they are weak and can do nothing to protect themselves.
- Now this what the Holy Spirit does, Satan cannot win over the Holy Spirit.
- (Mark 16:17-18 ERV) [17] And the people who believe will be able to do these things as proof: They will use my name to force demons out of people. They will speak in languages they never learned. [18] If they pick up snakes or drink any poison, they will not be hurt. They will lay their hands on sick people, and they will get well.”
- After studying these lessons, you will be able to do more than what is written there, you will get dreams of what is to happen, you will get visions on various circumstances, you will be able to see in the lives of anyone who asks you a question, all kinds of sickness and diseases will obey you.
