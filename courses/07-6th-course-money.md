### 6th Course: Money


#### 1st Topic: Accounts


**1.6.1.1 If you see money, it means we are soon ending our 1st Class. Leaving out money is something weird, it is not normal, but how does money relate with the Spirit, you will know at the end of these teachings. Accounts is a topic that looks at a person's financial status. In Accounts, you must write there all your inputs, inputs is the money which you get, output is the money which you spend. Still in the Accounts spreadsheet, you must write there and monitor all the money you get and all the money you spend. Every coin you get, it must be written in the Accounts file. I told you earlier that Spiritual Science is about calculations, laws, theories and principals, so even 1 USD left out can change the all calculations. You people don't understand that you have various Spirits in you.**

**1.6.1.1 In the Accounts spreadsheet there should be a section for GTN, GTN is Give The Needy, GTN is very important in calculations and in Spiritual Science, GTN is the fraction of the money you give to everyone who needs and to those who ask from you, for example, if you take 1/10 from all your inputs to the GTN, then you have a GTN of 0.100. That is a small value compared to the full value of 1, surely I say to you, if you have a GTN of 1, you can say to God, come down here, and He will descend, but having a GTN of 1 means giving all the money that you get to the needy. These things are more studied in the upper classes, this class is just an introduction to the true Spiritual Science.**

**1.6.1.3 So early, create the Spreadsheet for the Accounts topic.**

#### 2nd Course (Accounts Scale of preference)


**1.6.2.1 Accounts Scale of Preferences (ASP) is a joint file with that Accounts file. Account Scale of Preferences (ASP) is about the things that a person purchases. In this file is where people write whatever they would like to buy and whatever they want to buy regardless whether they will manage the costs or not. Anything that a person wants to buy, should first be written in this file. ASP is used in measuring wealth, so if you miss to write what you buy in this file, then you all system will be wrong.**

**1.6.2.2 You create a spreadsheet, name it Account Scales of Preferences, or any name that means the same meaning, but make sure you don't get confused during the calculation part. You may decide to add there sections or groupings according to your organization, what is required is that you have the Account Scales of Preferences file and that you are writing there the required information, other things shall be studied more in he 2nd class.**
