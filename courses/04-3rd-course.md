**1.3.2.1 Abilities are special thing that a certain person can do when other people can not do those things. Abilities are in the spirit not in the body, because all people have similar bodies but different spirits, for example, Jesus had an ability of healing people. An ability is something that makes you unique in a specific field or society of activity, for example, if you are a teacher, an ability is something that makes you different from other teachers, it is that thing that you know you have and you use it effectively and other teachers don't have it, instead other teachers just say, how do you do it. You have heard people saying, "how did he do it", it is the ability in his/her spirit that makes them to do what other people think are impossible to do.**

**1.3.2.2. For example, one of my abilities are waking up at 4:00 AM everyday, thinking about Bible Verses, and many more, these abilities make me unique from a crowd, and they help me in knowing God more. Abilities save days, they make life easy, and they help one live a steady life just like how the Daily Schedule does it. There are a lot of Abilities, and these are determined by factors like age, gender, society, relationship status (married & Single), financial status (rich or poor), but remember no rich person will ever enter the kingdom of God state of mind. All these determine the Abilities that a particular person can have. Now, to get started, think of Abilities that can solve particular problems in your day, Abilities are in the spirit, so it is God's Spirit in us which makes us able to be with those Abilities, you personally can do nothing, but God's Spirit in you will make you fit for every ability that you wish. When you need something you run after it, in the same way, you must run for Abilities if you really need them, you must be strong, though they sometimes look difficult at first, but soon you will get familiar with them.**

**1.3.2.3. Here is how you start, you create a spreadsheet, name it abilities, the sheets are the Abilities, every sheet must have its own ability. Or, if you are traditional, you can write your Abilities in a book, every ability must have its own page. Remember to pray to God for these Abilities, in the "Needs" file, you must write in things like these, asking God to make you able for every ability that you want.**
