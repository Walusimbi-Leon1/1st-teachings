The Daily Schedule is measured in “DS”, which means Daily Schedule. #### 2nd Topic: Thoughts


2.2.2.1 Thoughts is another file that is also important. In thoughts, it is like a voice telling you, “What if you did it yesterday?”. In the thoughts file, we write their every thought that we think of doing. Everything that you think of doing, should first be written in the “Thoughts file”. I am talking about anything that you may think of doing. Blessed is the man who thinks about nothing, he will enjoy this world. “Th” is the symbol for Thoughts, Th=TDC/TTh, where, Th is Thoughts, TDC is Thoughts Duration Completed, and TTh is Total Thoughts. Thoughts (Th) equals to Thoughts Duration Completed (TDC) divide by Total Thoughts (TTh). Thoughts Duration Completed (TDC) is the Thoughts Duration time, which is 48 hours (2 days). I believe that two days change the external, while the internal needs only 1 day to be changed. Now we know that Thoughts (TTh) = TDC/TTh, Let us use this in an example; Let’s say you have a total of five thoughts in you spreadsheet, and on those five thoughts, you only waited for the thought duration to be completed for only three thoughts; therefore, TTh=5, and TDC=3, Thoughts (Th)=3/5; =0.6 Th.
