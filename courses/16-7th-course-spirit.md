### 7th Course: Spirit


#### 1st Topic: Life


2.7.1.1 Concerning Life, the Life File, it is the collection of all features, what are these features, as written, that features cause abilities, so from all the thirteen Files, we are only collecting features, gaining more and more features, so that we gain more and more abilities, more than any other man on earth, and more than any other human being. The Life File has five worksheets, and these are; References, Sin, Fruits, Gifts, and Holy Spirit, these are the five worksheets of the Life File, In the References worksheet, this is where all calculations within the Life File are collected from, but before we continue with the References Worksheet, Let us first look at the other worksheets. Let us start with the Sin Worksheet, in the worksheet, It is where sins and righteousness are calculated from, on every day, you must calculate your sin and righteousness, and this is how to do it. In column A, you write there the date, in column B, you write there the 1st sin, in column C, you write there the 2nd sin, in column D, you write there the 3rd sin, like that, you continue writing in your usual sins until you see that they are almost done, these are the sins that you usually do, or the ones you do every day. So you must see that every time you do a sin, you write a 1 under that column having that sin, on the day when you don’t do that sin, you don’t write there anything, so that when calculating to get the TSi, you use this formula; =SUM(B187:M187), the equation for Sin is Si=TSi2/40, Si means Sin, TSi means Total sins, and 40 is a constant, for example according to Leon January 29, 2026, Leon had done 5 sins, for us to get the actual Sin  value, we use the Sin Formula; Si=TSi2/40,  we get 5^2, and this gives us 25,then 25/40, we get 0.625 Si, this means that on January 29, 2026, Leon had a sin value of 0.625 Si, and this means Leon had righteousness of 0.375 Ri, note that Righteousness is obtained by subtracting the value of Sin from 1, like; our sin was 0.625 Si, when we subtract 1-0.625 =0.375 Ri, and that is how we obtain Righteousness and sin.


Let us now look in the Fruits worksheet, There are nine fruits; and these include; Love, Joy, Peace, Patience, Kindness, Goodness, Faithfulness, Gentleness, and Self-control. These are the fruits of the Holy Spirit, and each of them is used to obtain features and become more abiitious than other human beings. We are going to start with Fruits itself; Fruits is the value of the sum of all the fruits, the formula for Fruits is Fr=(LV+Jy+Pe+Pa+Ki+Gd+Fa+Ge+Sc)/TF, here; Fr means Fruits, LV means Love, Jy means Joy, Pe means Peace, Pa means Patience, KI means Kindness, Gd means Goodness, Fa means Faithfulness, Ge means Gentleness, Sc means Self-control. From the formula for Fruits; we see that we add all the values from the fruits themselves.
