### 4th Course (Humans)


#### 1st Topic (Tests)


**1.4.1.1. Since we are all humans, we all face similar troubles and challenges, even though some of these challenges are determined by various factors, we all count it on God as the causer. I laugh at people who lament and cry because of their problems, yes their problems are true, and the problems really exist, but the reason why I laugh it is because people themselves cause those problems. I tried to put it to God Himself at first, but it was a great lie and a complex equation to solve, therefore, I declared two things, "Personal tests", and People tests". Here is where we get the Name " Tests". If you carefully look at everything done by Humans, they are all tests, God Himself tested when He created the earth, the earth was not there, so God took a broad test and He created the earth, not knowing what will come as a result. So it is the same with us humans, but our testing as humans is so severe, that a person can carry out more than 100 Tests in a single day, that is like saving 100 weapons to attack God. No one can leave testing, but we can all limit testing, if we limit testing, we limit the challenges and problems in our lives.**

**1.4.1.2. Since all things exist by "testing", we can not denay testing, but testing should not be a burden, it should not be a sin, but it should be a will of God, the Abilities we receive, all come through testing, but also sin and bad acts come through testing. When you test sinning for the first time, you are more likely to do that sin again, so this Topic totally restricts us from testing bad things, and in most things, somethings are bad but we don't know that they are bad.**

**1.4.1.3. Just like all other Topics, you need to write down the tests that you do, everyday. All this is begotten from Daily Schedule, when you don't follow your Daily Schedule very well, you might find yourself doing other things which you never even included in your "Thoughts file", tests that should be done, should be those ones written first in the Thoughts file, if not, those tests are sinful passions that rise from our true nature.**

**1.4.1.4. The "People Tests" are those tests caused by people, these tests don't come from a person's Spirit, but they come from the people, so these tested can be restricted by avoiding meeting people, not even hearing from people, They can only be controlled by keeping yourself away from people. Some of these People Tests include; People's Opinions, People's Advice, People's Instructions, and others, All this is considered no sense because a person with the Holy Spirit must be above reproach, no one must advise them, instruct them, or give them opinion, no one can advise a god, so everyone with the Holy Spirit is a god, and no one can advise that person.**

**1.4.1.5. "Personal Tests" are Tests that a person's Spirit start itself, they are not easy to control, they are like habits, they can cause depression, anxiety, and every emotion that feels worst.**

**1.4.1.6 So start now with the "Tests" knowledge, write every test that bothers you, anything that may seem new to you, any disturbances. Things that you start yourself are called "Personal Tests", then Tests from people are called " People Tests".**

#### 2nd Topic: Days


**1.4.2.1 Time is something greater than all things. Without time there exists nothing. We have considered Time in almost every File that we have looked at, but I further defined it into its separate File called "Days". Days is a simple File that shows us special events and also help in controlling our daily activities. As time goes on, you will see that it's a wonderful File worthy engaging with.**

**1.4.2.2 Days is categoriesied into "Special Days": These are Days which a person finds meaningful in life, they are very different from Public days, these Special Days are celebrated every after 40 days. We believe that a year is too long to remember something, plus, 40 works as a sign before God, so that is how these Special Days should celebrated. These days are mainly personal, a person celebrates them to reflect on moment in time that they went through.**

**1.4.2.3. Another section in the "Days" file is the calendar. This calendar helps us in marking and noticing the Special Days and other noticeable Days that we may not want to miss, so a calendar should also be made in the "Days" file.**

**1.4.2.4. other sheets can be created in this file like "Everyday", this is where various tasks and activities in specific days are written. And many sections can be got from the Days file. I don't limit or deny any addition that may be done on this, knowing this first, that all things can be modified or amended.**

**1.4.2.5. You need to create a spreadsheet, called Days, Make Sheets for Special Days, Calendar, Everyday, you can add also other sections.**
