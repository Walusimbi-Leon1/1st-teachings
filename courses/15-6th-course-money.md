#### 2nd Topic: Accounts Scale of preference


In the Accounts Scale of preference (ASP), there is one worksheet and it is called ASP, in this worksheet, it is where we write the desires and the it is where we do the calculations from. The Formula for the Accounts Scale of preference (ASP); is; WH = PC/TP, WH means wealth, PC means Purchase completed, TP means Total Purchases, Purchase Completed (PC) are the purchases you have completed making, Total Purchases (TP) is the overall number of purchases that you have on your list. Every purchase item has to have a specific identifier, and this how Desires are written, in column A, you write there the Identifier, in column B, you write there the Desire, in column C, you write there the Date; this is the date you have thought about purchasing that desire, in column D, you write there the Cost of that Desire, in column E, you write there the Purchase status, it is either 1 or 0, this is the value used in the calculation for wealth, you should know that Accounts Scale of preference (ASP) is not a feature; the feature is Wealth, and this is what we need to unlock more abilities, then in column F, you write there the Purchase Date, this is the date when you purchased that specific Desire. The Identifiers should be written from A1 to A2, like that. For example, according to Leon January 29, 2026, Leon had a Purchase Completed (PC) of 19, and Total Purchases (TP) of 74, so this would be 19/74 = 0.257 WH, WH is the unit symbol of wealth, so this means that on January 29, 2026, Leon had a Wealth of 0.257 WH.
