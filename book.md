**2.7.1.6. The Holy Spirit worksheet is the most important worksheet in the Life File. In this worksheet, you track your relationship with the Holy Spirit Himself. This is not about formulas or calculations. This is about your personal walk with God. Here is how to set it up:**

**In the Holy Spirit worksheet, create these columns:**
- **Column A — Date:** The date of the entry.
- **Column B — Holy Spirit Experience:** Write down any experience you had with the Holy Spirit that day. Did you feel His presence? Did He speak to you? Did He guide you in a decision?
- **Column C — Scripture:** What Bible verse did the Holy Spirit use to speak to you today?
- **Column D — Obedience:** Did you obey what the Holy Spirit told you to do? Write Yes or No and explain.
- **Column E — Growth:** How did you grow closer to the Holy Spirit today? Write one sentence.

**The formula for the Holy Spirit is: HS = DO / TO, where HS means Holy Spirit, DO means Days of Obedience (days when you obeyed the Holy Spirit's leading), and TO means Total Days in the period you are measuring. For example, if in 30 days you obeyed the Holy Spirit 22 times, then HS = 22/30 = 0.733 HS.**

**Remember: the Holy Spirit is a person, not a force. You can grieve Him (Ephesians 4:30). You can quench Him (1 Thessalonians 5:19). You can be filled with Him (Ephesians 5:18). The goal of all these Thirteen Files is to prepare you to be a dwelling place for the Holy Spirit. Without the Holy Spirit, all these files are just empty spreadsheets. But with the Holy Spirit, they become the very breath of God in your life.**

**2.7.1.7. Now let us return to the References worksheet. In References, we collect all the values from Sin, Fruits, Gifts, and Holy Spirit worksheets and we calculate the overall Life value. The formula for Life is: Li = (Ri + Fr + Gi + HS) / 4, where Li means Life, Ri means Righteousness (which is 1 - Sin), Fr means Fruits, Gi means Gifts, and HS means Holy Spirit.**

**For example, if on a specific day:**
- Ri = 0.375 (from Sin of 0.625)
- Fr = 0.589
- Gi = 0.333
- HS = 0.733

**Then Li = (0.375 + 0.589 + 0.333 + 0.733) / 4 = 2.030 / 4 = 0.508 Li. The units of Life are Li.**

**A Life value above 0.700 means you are living a Spirit-filled life. A Life value below 0.400 means you need to go back and work on your files. Remember: Life is the overall file. It represents YOU. So make sure your Life value is always growing, never decreasing.**

### Summary of All Thirteen Files

**3.1.1. We have now come to the end of this book. But the end of this book is not the end of your journey. It is the beginning. Let us take a moment to remember what we have learned and how each of the Thirteen Files works together to build your spiritual life.**

**The Thirteen Files are:**

1. **Daily Schedule (DS)** — The foundation. It orders your day and makes your life predictable and holy. A person without a Daily Schedule is like a house without a foundation.

2. **Thoughts (Th)** — The gateway. It controls what enters your mind and gives you time to examine every thought before you act on it. A person who controls their thoughts controls their life.

3. **Needs (ND)** — The prayer list. It keeps track of everything you ask God for, so you can see when He answers. This builds your faith and proves the existence of God.

4. **Abilities (Ab)** — The power. It develops the unique skills and gifts that God has placed in your spirit. Your abilities make you different from everyone else.

5. **Tests (Te)** — The mirror. It shows you your bad habits and the tests that come from people. By tracking tests, you reduce sin in your life.

6. **Days (Da)** — The calendar. It marks special days and helps you celebrate what God has done in your life. It also tracks fasting and everyday events.

7. **Goals (Go)** — The engine. It breaks negative emotions and gives you something meaningful to work on. Goals make life exciting.

8. **Court (Co)** — The judge. It creates laws that govern your life and punishes you when you break them. Without the Court, you have no discipline.

9. **Personal Duties (PD)** — The body care. It makes sure you do what you must do to maintain your health and your environment.

10. **Accounts (Ac)** — The wallet. It tracks every coin that comes in and goes out. Money is spiritual, and how you handle it affects your spirit.

11. **Accounts Scale of Preferences (ASP)** — The desires. It lists everything you want to buy and tracks your wealth. Your desires show what is in your heart.

12. **Life (Li)** — The summary. It brings together Sin, Fruits, Gifts, and the Holy Spirit into one overall value. Life is who you are.

13. **Spiritual Teachings (ST)** — The wisdom. It records what the Holy Spirit teaches you personally. These teachings are unique to you.
