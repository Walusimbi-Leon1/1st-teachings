About This Book

SG Class Teachings is a spiritual growth guide about receiving and walking with the Holy Spirit. Begun June 29, 2025, it presents a structured system of spiritual disciplines designed to prepare a person to draw near to God.

The teachings are built around thirteen core files — digital spreadsheets that track and measure spiritual growth. Each file represents a dimension of life that, when properly ordered, brings a person into closer fellowship with the Holy Spirit.

This is not theory alone. It is a practical, measurable system that uses mathematics, scripture, and daily discipline to transform how a person lives, thinks, prays, and grows.

The Thirteen Files

These thirteen files form the structure of the spiritual life. They are the framework within which the Holy Spirit works.

📋 Daily Schedule — Daily routine & discipline
💭 Thoughts — Purifying your mind
🙏 Needs — Prayer & asking God
⚡ Abilities — Spiritual gifts & skills
🔬 Tests — Understanding life's challenges
📅 Days — Time, calendars & special days
🎯 Goals — Purpose & achievement
⚖️ Court — Self-governance & discipline
🧹 Personal Duties — Health & responsibility
💰 Accounts — Money management
📊 ASP — Scale of preference & wealth
🌿 Life — Fruits, Gifts & the Spirit
📜 Teachings — Words from the Holy Spirit
